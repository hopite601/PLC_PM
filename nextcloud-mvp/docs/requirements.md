# Yeu cau MVP

## Van de can giai quyet

Cong ty can quan ly du lieu tren dam may, chia se duoc cho nhan vien, tiet kiem chi phi va van dam bao an toan du lieu.

## Doi tuong su dung

- Admin he thong.
- Giam doc/quan ly.
- Ke toan.
- Nhan su.
- Kinh doanh.
- Nhan vien thong thuong.

## Chuc nang MVP

- Dang nhap bang tai khoan rieng cho tung nhan vien.
- Tao nhom theo phong ban.
- Tao thu muc dung chung theo phong ban.
- Upload/download file.
- Chia se file noi bo qua tai khoan/email nhan vien.
- Phan quyen xem/sua/xoa theo nhom.
- Khoi phuc file da xoa.
- Xem lich su hoat dong co ban.
- Dong bo file voi may tinh/di dong bang app Nextcloud.
- Backup du lieu va database.

## Chua lam trong MVP

- Email server rieng.
- Tich hop SSO/LDAP.
- Ma hoa dau cuoi cho tung file.
- Tu dong phan loai tai lieu.
- Quy trinh phe duyet tai lieu phuc tap.

## Tieu chi MVP dat yeu cau

- 5-10 nguoi dung thu nghiem dang nhap duoc.
- Upload/download file on dinh.
- Nguoi phong ban nay khong xem duoc thu muc phong ban khac neu khong duoc cap quyen.
- Admin khoi phuc duoc file da xoa.
- Backup tao ra duoc ban sao du lieu va database.
- Nguoi dung binh thuong hieu cach dung sau 15-30 phut huong dan.
