## Kich ban test

1. Admin tao tai khoan va gan nhom cho tung nguoi.
2. Admin tao cac thu muc mau va chia se theo `permission-matrix.csv`.
3. Ke toan upload mot file hoa don mau vao `03_Ke_toan`.
4. Sale thu truy cap `03_Ke_toan`, ket qua mong doi la khong thay hoac khong co quyen mo.
5. Sale upload bao gia mau vao `05_Kinh_doanh`.
6. Giam doc xem thu muc ke toan va kinh doanh.
7. Nhan vien xem `01_Tai_lieu_chung` nhung khong sua duoc neu chi cap quyen View.
8. Admin xoa mot file test, sau do khoi phuc tu thung rac.
9. Mot nguoi dung cai app Nextcloud Desktop va thu dong bo mot thu muc nho.
10. Admin chay backup thu nghiem va ghi lai thoi gian khoi phuc.

- Dang nhap co de khong?
